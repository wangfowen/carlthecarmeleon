Carl

Carl the Carmelon is the king of all chameleons. Carl has the ability to change colours and absorbs all who pass in his way. On his excursion eating combustible propane crickets, he has grown a fiery spine. From the gases of his many meals, Carl has the ability to vibrate the air around his body in patterns humans would call music. Join Carl the Carmeleon for an interactive light and fire show, and for a chocolate cricket treat!
